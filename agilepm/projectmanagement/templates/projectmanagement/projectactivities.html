Project Activities - Role of project management team

The role of project management team is to:

  • Plan
  • Delegate
  • Monitor
  • Control and report progress

There should be a minimum of two people in a project team (project executive and project manager). Roles include - project manager, team manager, project executive, project support, senior user(s).

Role Responsibilities
Executive
  • Oversees business case
  • Appoints project manager
  • Key decision maker
  • Accountable for project success
Project manager
  • Day to day management
  • Lead and motivate project team
  • Authorise work packages
  • Manages project delivery
Senior user
  • Specify operational and maintenance needs
  • Define acceptance criteria
  • Monitor products against requirements
  • Ensure benefits are realised
Senior supplier
  • Realistic design proposals
  • Quality of products
  • Confirm viability of project approach
  • Maintenance of specialist products after closure

Business case

In Prince2 all projects must have a documented business justification. A business case is developed at the beginning of a project and provides the cost, benefits, expected benefits, risk and timescales. It confirms whether the project is desirable, viable and achievable.

The business case is reviewed and updated as it develops and evolves and formally verified by the project board at each key decision point.

Project planning

  • Design a plan (project plan (high level), detailed level plans - stage plan, team plan (optional), exception plan)
  • Define and analyse the products
  • Identify activities and dependancies
  • Prepare estimates
  • Prepare a schedule
  • Analyse risks to the plan
  • Document and publish the plan

Measuring performance

The six aspects of performance are

  • Cost
  • Time
  • Quality
  • Scope
  • Benefits
  • Risk

These aspects are measurable and can therefore be managed. Any of these aspects could change which would in turn affect the other aspects.

Risk

Risk is an uncertain event or set of events that should it occur will have an effect on achievement of objectives.

Issue is something that has or will happen. (If a risk is not dealt with it becomes an issue.)

Prince2 minimum requirements in relation to risk are

  • Define risk management approach
  • Maintain some form of risk register
  • Ensure that project risks are:
    • identified (lessons log),
    • assessed (probability and proximity),
    • managed (plan mitigations) and
    • reviewed (establish owner) throughout the project lifecycle
  • Use lessons to inform risk identification and management

Make sure there is good communication between risk owners, project manager and stakeholders
